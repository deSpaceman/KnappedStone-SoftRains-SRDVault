### Blink

*Keywords*: Crafting Complexity Expert

*Cost*: 1 Souls
*Test*: Focus (Difficulty 2)

The User teleports, up to their [[2.4.1 - Attributes|Movement]], to an empty [[5.2 - The Grid|Gridspace]] that they are aware of. Using Blink counts as Moving, but it does not trigger [[5.3.2 - Action Economy|Opportunity Attacks]].
