### Miraclework

*Keywords*:

*Mantra Difficulty*: 4
*Test*: Focus (Difficulty 4)

The User focus their beliefs and tugs into the very lattice that makes up the world. While doing so, they may make a wish in the form of a single phrase, with no if statements. How the wish is realized is up to Worldshaper discretion, but the User must be mindful, because some wishes have [[Revival Attrition|dire prices...]]
